The Curious Case of College Sports' Spending Spree

Despite often being thought of as a million-dollar industry, the truth is that as a whole, college athletics are not very profitable. Most Division 1 programs in the United States do not even turn a profit; only 25 D1 schools earned net revenue in 2019. So why is this the case? Simple: they're paying a whole lot more than they used to.
